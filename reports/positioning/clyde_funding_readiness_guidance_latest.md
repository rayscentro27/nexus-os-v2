# Clyde Funding Readiness Guidance

Clyde is framed as a Funding Readiness Guide. Guidance now explains what may affect funding readiness, connects Credit Profile and Business Profile work to Tier 1 and Tier 2 positioning, and describes report-item review in terms of inaccurate, outdated, duplicated, unfamiliar, incomplete, or unverifiable information.

Clyde may organize documents and present options. Clyde does not claim to verify report items, repair credit, remove items, increase a score, or guarantee funding. GoClear review and client choice remain required where applicable.
