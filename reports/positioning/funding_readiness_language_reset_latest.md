# Funding Readiness Language Reset

Nexus is now presented as **Nexus Funding Readiness**: a membership that helps clients organize and optimize their Credit Profile and Business Profile for stronger Tier 1 and Tier 2 business funding positioning.

Applied client language includes Credit Profile Optimization, Credit Report Review Tools, funding-impact items, utilization strategy, documentation options, GoClear Review, Draft Letter Tools, and Documents Vault. The Funding Readiness page explicitly connects Credit Profile + Business Profile and states that lenders make final approval decisions.

Applied admin language includes Credit & Funding Readiness Review, Review Queue, Profile Review Cases, Report Analysis, Report Items, Draft Letters, and Mail Queue. Parser counts are shown as funding-impact items rather than negative candidates.

The canonical terms and disclaimers live in `src/content/nexusPositioning.ts`. The compatibility route and legacy internal identifiers remain intentionally unchanged.

Core safety statement: Nexus provides education, organization tools, and funding-readiness workflows. It does not guarantee credit score increases, item removals, or funding approval.
