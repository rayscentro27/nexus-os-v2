# Simple One-Document Upload Flow

- In-page upload panel added: `True`
- One document at a time: `True`
- Upload redirects removed from main pages: `True`
- Documents remains vault: `True`

## Flow

Client opens a page, Clyde recommends the next action, the client uploads one document from that page, Nexus stores it in `client_documents`, and the UI shows Pending GoClear Review.

## Pages With Central Upload Lane

- Home
- Credit Profile
- Business Profile
- Business Setup compatibility route
- Business Funding
- Request Review
- Credit Repair Journey
- Dispute Review support documents
