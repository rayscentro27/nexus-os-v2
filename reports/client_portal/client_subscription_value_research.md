# Client Subscription Value Research

**Generated:** 2026-07-05  
**Status:** Research Complete  

## Core Finding

Credit/business funding clients pay monthly for **ongoing value**, not one-time access. The portal must deliver continuous, actionable benefits that justify recurring fees.

## Value Drivers

### 1. Ongoing Value

- Monthly score updates with trend analysis
- New funding opportunities discovered regularly
- Grant matches refreshed based on business changes
- Seasonal action items (tax deadlines, renewal dates)

### 2. Readiness Score

- Dynamic score that updates as documents/tasks complete
- Breakdown of factors affecting score
- Actionable steps to improve score
- Comparison to funding requirements

### 3. Business Setup Guidance

- EIN application status and next steps
- LLC formation tracking
- Business bank account setup
- Business credit card recommendations

### 4. Document Vault

- Secure storage for business documents
- Expiration alerts (license, insurance)
- Quick-access for funding applications
- Version history and audit trail

### 5. Monthly Action Plan

- Personalized to-do list for the month
- Priority-ranked tasks based on funding goals
- Deadline tracking
- Progress celebration (completion streaks)

### 6. Funding/Grant Updates

- New matching grants discovered
- Application status tracking
- Deadline reminders
- Success rate insights by funding type

### 7. Hermes Guidance

- AI-powered recommendations
- Context-aware suggestions
- Answer funding questions
- Guide through application processes

### 8. Review Requests

- Automated Google review requests
- Review response templates
- Reputation monitoring
- Impact on funding eligibility

## Pricing Psychology

| Tier | Price | Justification |
|------|-------|---------------|
| Starter | $29/mo | Score + docs + basic funding |
| Growth | $79/mo | Everything + grants + team |
| Agency | $149/mo | Multi-client + billing |

### Why Clients Stay

1. **Switching cost**: Documents, history, progress locked in
2. **Fear of missing**: New grants, score changes, deadlines
3. **Habit**: Monthly check-ins become routine
4. **Trust**: Relationship with Hermes AI
5. **Results**: Funding secured = lifetime value proven

## Churn Risk Factors

- No new value delivered month-to-month
- Score stagnation
- No funding matches found
- Poor support experience
- Price increase without feature addition

## Retention Strategies

- Monthly "value report" showing what was gained
- New feature announcements
- Success story sharing
- Score improvement celebrations
- Anniversary rewards
