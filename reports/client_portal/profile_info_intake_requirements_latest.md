# Profile Info Intake Requirements

## Collected Fields

- Basic Identity: legal name, preferred name, email, phone, mailing address, city, state, postal code.
- Credit Report Access: report access status and inline credit report upload.
- Business Foundation: existing business status, legal name, DBA, entity type, state formed, formation date optional, address, phone, email, website, industry, NAICS optional.
- EIN / Entity Details: EIN status and optional EIN last 4 only.
- Funding Goals: amount/range, purpose, timeline, revenue range, time in business, preferred funding type.

## Not Collected

- SSN
- Full DOB
- Full EIN
- Bank account numbers
- Credit card numbers
- Credit bureau credentials

## Required Inline Documents

- Government ID
- Proof of Address
- Credit Report
- EIN Confirmation
- Business Formation Docs
- Business License
- Bank Statement
- Tax Return
- P&L when required
