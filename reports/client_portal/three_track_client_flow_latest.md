# Three-Track Client Flow

## Credit Profile

- Upload credit report.
- Choose items to challenge.
- Review utilization improvement options.
- Upload evidence.
- Review/approve letters after specialist review.
- Track outcomes.

## Business Profile

- Complete personal/contact basics.
- Complete business basics.
- Add EIN/entity status without full EIN collection.
- Add business address, phone, website, banking status, DUNS/business profile state.
- Upload business documents inline.

## Business Funding

- Review funding readiness snapshot.
- Upload missing funding documents.
- Understand Credit Profile and Business Profile impact.
- Review funding option categories.
- Request GoClear funding review.
