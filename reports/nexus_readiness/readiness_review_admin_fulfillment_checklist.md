# $97 Credit & Funding Readiness Review — Admin Fulfillment Checklist

**Purpose:** Step-by-step checklist for Ray to fulfill the $97 readiness review manually
**Usage:** Follow this checklist for each new $97 client
**Time estimate:** 30-60 minutes per client

---

## Pre-Fulfillment

- [ ] Confirm payment received (manual payment, Stripe test, or manual approval)
- [ ] Log payment in manual tracking spreadsheet
- [ ] Create Ray Review card: "New $97 Readiness Review — [CLIENT NAME]"

---

## Step 1: Collect Intake

- [ ] Send intake questions to client (conversation, Google Form, or Typeform)
- [ ] Confirm client has provided answers for all required sections
- [ ] Follow up on any missing required information
- [ ] Save intake responses to manual notes

**Intake sections to confirm complete:**
- [ ] Personal credit readiness
- [ ] Credit report availability
- [ ] Negative items
- [ ] Credit utilization
- [ ] Inquiries
- [ ] Collections/charge-offs
- [ ] Credit goals
- [ ] Business entity status
- [ ] EIN / DUNS / SOS / NAICS
- [ ] Business address / phone / email / website
- [ ] Business bank account
- [ ] Business credit monitoring
- [ ] Funding goal
- [ ] Timeline
- [ ] Documents available

---

## Step 2: Collect Credit Report or Details

- [ ] Ask client to provide credit report or scores
- [ ] If client cannot provide, note this in the report
- [ ] Record credit score range from client-provided data
- [ ] Record any negative items client reports
- [ ] Record utilization from client-provided data

---

## Step 3: Review Credit Readiness

- [ ] Score Credit Profile section (0-100)
- [ ] Score Utilization section (0-100)
- [ ] Score Negative Items section (0-100)
- [ ] Score Inquiries section (0-100)
- [ ] Identify credit-level flags (cleanup needed, utilization too high, etc.)

---

## Step 4: Review Business Funding Readiness

- [ ] Score Business Foundation section (0-100)
- [ ] Score Business Bankability section (0-100)
- [ ] Score Funding Documents section (0-100)
- [ ] Score Funding Timing section (0-100)
- [ ] Identify business-level flags (foundation gaps, bankability low, etc.)

---

## Step 5: Complete Scorecard

- [ ] Enter all section scores into the scorecard
- [ ] Calculate weighted overall score
- [ ] Determine readiness tier (Not Ready / Needs Cleanup / Almost Ready / Ready Starter / Ready Advanced)
- [ ] Document all flags and specific blockers

---

## Step 6: Draft Client Report

- [ ] Open client report template
- [ ] Fill in Executive Summary
- [ ] Fill in Readiness Score table
- [ ] Fill in Credit Profile Findings
- [ ] Fill in Business Funding Findings
- [ ] Fill in Top Blockers (3 most important)
- [ ] Fill in Recommended Next Steps (4 items)
- [ ] Fill in What to Fix First
- [ ] Fill in What to Avoid
- [ ] Fill in Suggested Upgrade Path
- [ ] Confirm disclaimer is included
- [ ] Review for tone (CEO/Jarvis style, plain language, no jargon)
- [ ] Review for accuracy (matches intake data)

---

## Step 7: Prepare Follow-Up Recommendation

- [ ] Determine which upgrade path fits the client:
  - $297 Credit Assistant Plan — for clients who want hands-on help
  - Monthly Readiness Subscription — for clients who want ongoing monitoring
  - No upgrade — for clients who prefer DIY
- [ ] Draft the recommendation language
- [ ] Confirm recommendation is ethical (no guaranteed outcomes)

---

## Step 8: Log Outcome in Ray Review

- [ ] Create or update Ray Review card with:
  - Client name
  - Readiness tier
  - Overall score
  - Top 3 blockers
  - Recommended next steps
  - Upgrade recommendation
  - Status: pending delivery
- [ ] Review and approve the card

---

## Step 9: Deliver to Client

- [ ] Send the completed report to client via conversation
- [ ] Confirm client received and understood the report
- [ ] Answer any initial questions
- [ ] Present the upgrade recommendation (if applicable)
- [ ] Log delivery in manual tracking

---

## Step 10: Follow-Up

- [ ] Set manual reminder to follow up in 7 days
- [ ] Check if client has questions or needs help
- [ ] If upgrade was recommended, follow up on interest
- [ ] If no upgrade, check in on progress
- [ ] Log follow-up in manual tracking

## Quality Checklist

Before delivering, confirm:

- [ ] Report is in CEO/Jarvis tone (plain language, practical)
- [ ] No jargon or technical dumps
- [ ] No guaranteed outcomes
- [ ] No fake live claims
- [ ] All scores match the scorecard
- [ ] Recommended steps are specific and actionable
- [ ] Disclaimer is included
- [ ] Upgrade recommendation is ethical and non-pushy
- [ ] Report is ready for client delivery
