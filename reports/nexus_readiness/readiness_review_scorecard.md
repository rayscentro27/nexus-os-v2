## Readiness Tiers

| Tier | Score Range | Label | Meaning |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 0-25 | **Not Ready** | Significant gaps in credit and/or business foundation. Must address basics before funding applications. |
| 2 | 26-45 | **Needs Cleanup First** | Partial foundation exists but negative items, utilization, or missing setup items block progress. Focus on cleanup. |
| 3 | 46-65 | **Almost Ready** | Core foundation in place. Address specific gaps and timing issues. 3-6 months of preparation recommended. |

## How to Use This Scorecard

1. **Collect intake** — use the client intake form
2. **Score each section** — fill in the score for each factor
3. **Calculate section totals** — convert to /100
4. **Apply weights** — multiply each section by its weight
5. **Sum weighted scores** — get overall score
6. **Determine tier** — match score to readiness tier
7. **Check flags** — identify specific areas needing attention
8. **Draft report** — use the client report template
9. **Review via Ray Review** — approve before delivering
10. **Deliver to client** — via conversation, with upgrade recommendation
