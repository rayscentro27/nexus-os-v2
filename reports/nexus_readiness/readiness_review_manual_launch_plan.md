| Metric | Target | How to Track |
|---|---|---|
| Time per review | Under 60 minutes | Manual timer |
| Client satisfaction | 4+ out of 5 | Follow-up question |
| Upgrade conversion | 20%+ | Track offers made vs accepted |
| Revenue per review | $97 (base) | Payment tracking |
| Revenue per upgraded client | $97 + $297 = $394 | Payment tracking |

---

## Safety Boundaries

During the manual launch:

- **No emails sent** — delivery via conversation only
- **No charges made** — client pays voluntarily
- **No publishing** — social posts are drafts until approved
- **No schedulers** — all follow-ups are manual reminders
- **No production data mutations** — all data is manually tracked
- **No approval bypass** — every report goes through Ray Review
- **No fake live claims** — all data is from client-provided intake
