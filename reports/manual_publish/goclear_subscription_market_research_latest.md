## Recommended GoClear tiers
### Credit Monitoring & Action Plan — ~$49/mo (range $39-$69)
- includes: credit report review, score tracking (SmartCredit), dispute guidance, monthly action plan, portal access, reminders
- retention: Ongoing monitoring + monthly steps keep progress visible.
- next tier: credit_plus_business_setup

### Credit + Business Setup — ~$97/mo (range $79-$129)
- includes: everything in Action Plan, business setup checklist, affiliate/DIY choices, letter packet tracking, document tracking, business credit guidance
- retention: Clients keep building business credit after personal credit improves.
- next tier: funding_readiness

### Funding Readiness — ~$197/mo (range $149-$297)
- includes: everything in Credit + Business Setup, funding readiness tracking, bankability scoring, vendor accounts guidance, funding preparation, Ray-approved funding path
- retention: Clients want to reach/maintain funding-ready status.
- next tier: post_funding_growth

### Post-Funding Growth — ~$149/mo (range $99-$249)
- includes: monthly financial readiness tracking, business credit building, vendor accounts, grant/funding opportunity monitoring, bankability maintenance
- retention: After funding, clients keep growing credit and monitoring new opportunities.
- next tier: none
