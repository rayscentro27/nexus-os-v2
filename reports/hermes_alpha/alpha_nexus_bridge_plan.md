# Hermes Alpha / Nexus Bridge Plan

Alpha owns public/document research, opportunity and affiliate scoring, audience/marketing drafts, trading research/backtest plans, and Alpha reports. Nexus owns customers, consent, client data, GoClear delivery, operational truth, payments, subscriptions, referrals, Ray Review records, and approved external execution.

Alpha may propose: campaign/asset drafts, opportunity experiments, affiliate candidates, landing/newsletter/social drafts, research summaries, and trading research reports. Every artifact carries ID/version, brand/audience, sources, assumptions, confidence, risk, external-action requirements, and proposed decision.

Ray Review is mandatory before promotion. Nexus accepts only a copied, immutable, approved artifact with receipt and explicit destination. It may reject stale evidence, missing sources, wrong brand, prohibited claims, client-data assumptions, or unclear ownership.

Never cross automatically: raw client data, credentials, payment state, subscriber lists, client communications, Alpha memory, untrusted prompts, broker instructions, orders, publish/send commands, schedulers, or database writes.

Examples:

1. Alpha drafts a GoClear campaign → Ray reviews exact copy/assets/CTA → approved artifact enters Nexus marketing queue → Nexus/authorized human handles lead capture, payment, onboarding, and delivery.
2. Alpha creates trading research → Ray may approve a summary for Nexus display → Nexus cannot trade from it; any future practice proposal follows a separate trading gate.

Phase 1 bridge is a report/contract only. There is no code path, database, event bus, or shared memory.
