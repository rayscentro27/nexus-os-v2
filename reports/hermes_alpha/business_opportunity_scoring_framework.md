# Business Opportunity Scoring Framework

Score each 0–100, then apply weights:

| Dimension | Weight | Evidence standard |
|---|---:|---|
| Client demand/problem severity | 20% | Direct questions, search/market evidence, or approved interviews |
| Revenue potential | 15% | Transparent price × plausible volume range; no certainty claim |
| Speed to manual launch | 15% | Days to a reversible test, not full automation |
| Low cost to test | 10% | Cash/tool/time cost with zero-cost alternative |
| Nexus/strategic fit | 10% | Reuses capabilities without confusing ownership |
| Differentiation/proof | 10% | Specific advantage and evidence needed |
| Affiliate/referral potential | 5% | Verified program/terms/disclosure only |
| Operational feasibility | 10% | Fulfillment, skills, dependencies, support |
| Compliance/safety | 5% | Higher score means lower risk and clear controls |

Total bands: 80–100 propose controlled test; 65–79 research/validate; 45–64 hold; below 45 reject. Automatic blockers override score: deception, guaranteed financial outcomes, unlicensed code use, prohibited platform automation, uncontrolled spend, client-data exposure, or irreversible external action.

Every score records source, date, assumptions, confidence, sensitivity, next experiment, maximum test cost, success/stop criteria, and Ray Review status.
