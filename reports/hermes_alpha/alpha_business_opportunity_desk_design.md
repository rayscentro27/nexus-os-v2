# Alpha Business Opportunity Desk Design

Input: validated Research Intake artifacts plus Ray's objective. Output:

- opportunity summary and target user/problem
- monetization category and offer hypothesis
- revenue potential (hypothesis, not forecast)
- speed to launch and cost to test
- demand evidence and missing proof
- Nexus fit and credit/funding relevance
- affiliate/referral potential and disclosure needs
- compliance/platform/dependency risk
- confidence and assumptions
- smallest reversible next experiment
- conversation-only Ray Review proposal

Workflow: qualify source → deduplicate → map audience/problem → score → challenge assumptions → design manual experiment → identify kill criteria → draft proposal. No checkout, lead contact, customer record, payment, referral link, or automated experiment.
