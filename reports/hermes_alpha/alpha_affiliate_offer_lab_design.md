# Alpha Affiliate / Offer Lab Design

The lab researches programs and composes offers; it never applies, activates links, captures leads, charges, or fulfills.

Record: audience/problem, core offer, free/DIY alternative, partner/program, official terms URL/date, eligibility, commission hypothesis, cookie/attribution, prohibited claims, disclosure, platform restrictions, brand fit, landing assets, checkout handoff, lead-capture needs, campaign drafts, compliance review, confidence, and next experiment.

Offer stacks separate owned service value from third-party referral value. Referral economics never outrank client fit. Unknown/current terms block promotion. Every draft includes material-connection disclosure and a non-affiliate option.

Ray Review proposal: evidence, offer, audience, expected value range, maximum test cost, operational owner, landing/CTA draft, compliance risks, success/stop criteria, and explicit requested decision. Nexus owns approved landing deployment, lead capture, checkout/payment, subscriptions, customer records, and delivery.
