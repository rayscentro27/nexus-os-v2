# Alpha Marketing Department / Asset Studio Design

## Audience and brand split

- Nexus/GoClear: consumers and small-business owners seeking educational credit/funding readiness. Trust, privacy, compliance, and human fulfillment dominate.
- Hermes Alpha: builders/operators interested in research, opportunity experiments, marketing systems, and trading research. Evidence, experiment design, and risk dominate.

Use separate brand profiles, calendars, claims libraries, CTAs, landing domains/sections, and ideally separate social accounts. A shared production team is acceptable; shared audience memory and autoposting are not.

## Rooms and workflow

Campaign Strategy → Content Queue → Creative/Image Studio → Landing Page → Newsletter → Social Scheduler (plan-only) → Auto-Response Inbox (plan-only) → Metrics Dashboard → Low-Cost Growth Research.

Each campaign has brand, audience, objective, source evidence, offer, channel, assets, CTA, compliance notes, budget cap, owner, status, review receipt, metrics, and stop criteria. States: research, draft, factual review, Ray Review, approved-for-manual-use, externally executed by an authorized system, measured, archived.

Creative workflow stores prompt, references, model/tool/license, dimensions, alt text, claims, review, and output hash. Landing/newsletter/social flows use brand templates and require factual/compliance review. Auto-response remains a response library and escalation design—no actual replies.

Metrics: qualified conversations, intake starts, completion, paid conversions verified by Nexus, fulfillment time, refunds, content engagement, landing conversion, unsubscribe/complaint, cost, and experiment confidence. Alpha reports interpretation and next test to Ray; Nexus owns customer/payment facts.

Zero/low-cost research: approved warm-contact interviews, FAQ mining, public competitor/keyword analysis, manual post tests, lead magnets, partner interviews, and small copy variants. No scraping abuse, bought lists, fake engagement, or unreviewed claims.

All sends, publishing, scheduling activation, auto-replies, and spend require Ray Review and occur outside Alpha.
