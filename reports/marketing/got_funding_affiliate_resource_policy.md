## Disclosure Requirements

- All affiliate content must include clear "affiliate link" disclosure **above the fold**
- Disclosure must be in plain language: *"This page contains affiliate links. We may earn a commission if you purchase through these links, at no extra cost to you."*
- Disclosure must appear **before** any affiliate link on the page
- Social media posts must include `#affiliate` or `#ad` in the first line
- Video content must include verbal disclosure in the first 30 seconds

## Placement Rules

- Maximum 3 affiliate links per content page
- No affiliate links in email subject lines
- Affiliate links must use `rel="nofollow sponsored"` attributes
- No affiliate links on checkout or pricing pages
- Affiliate CTAs must not conflict with primary Nexus OS CTAs
- Maximum 1 affiliate link per social media post

## Compliance

- All affiliate links tracked in `affiliate_links` registry
- Monthly audit of affiliate link performance and compliance
- Affiliates must be vetted for alignment with Nexus values
- No promotion of competing products/services
- FTC compliance required — no deceptive claims
- Affiliate revenue tracked separately in billing module
- Ray Review required before any new affiliate is approved
