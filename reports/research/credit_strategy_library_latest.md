# Credit Strategy Library

- 25 initial versioned strategies.
- Client states: `approved_for_education` and `approved_for_tool_use` only.
- Lifecycle: discovered → review → conditional/approved/rejected/retired.
- Approval is reusable per strategy version, not repeated per client.
- Definitions cover applicability, exclusions, report/client facts, evidence, tools, outcomes, limits, risks, and prohibited claims.
- Official support: https://www.consumerfinance.gov/compliance/circulars/consumer-financial-protection-circular-2022-07-reasonable-investigation-of-consumer-reporting-disputes/ and https://www.consumerfinance.gov/rules-policy/regulations/1006/34/ .
