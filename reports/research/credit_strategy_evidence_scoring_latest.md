# Credit Strategy Evidence Scoring

The deterministic scorer weights official authority, statutes/regulations, agency and bureau guidance, internal evidence, recency, specificity, applicability, deceptive wording, and client-fact dependency. Practitioner evidence has low weight. Views, testimonials, “hack” labels, or repository existence are not approval evidence.

Outputs: evidence score, risk score, confidence, and tool/education/conditional/reject recommendation. High deceptive-wording risk forces rejection.
