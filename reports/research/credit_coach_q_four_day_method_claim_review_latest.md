# Credit Coach Q Method Claim Review

Classification: practitioner/promotional discovery; not authoritative by itself.

Eligible leads: three-bureau comparison; balance, date, masked-fragment, and status comparison; original-creditor information; ownership and chain-of-title documentation; debt-validation education; certified-mail tracking; staged follow-up; evidence organization.

Eight rejected claims: four-day removal, universal 87% results, blanket disputes, universal original-signed-contract requirement, automatic deletion after nonproduction, automatic $1,000 damages, mandatory compliance-manager deletion, and guaranteed outcomes.

Approved education is limited to objective comparison, accurate fact-specific investigation requests, evidence organization, tracking, and conditional original-creditor/validation education. CFPB dispute guidance: https://www.consumerfinance.gov/ask-cfpb/how-do-i-dispute-an-error-on-my-credit-report-en-314/ . Regulation F: https://www.consumerfinance.gov/rules-policy/regulations/1006/34/ . Blanket application is prohibited.
