# Cross-Bureau Discrepancy Engine

Accounts group only with a masked suffix plus support, or furnisher + type + opened date. Similar names alone never merge. Records preserve masked bureau values and confidence.

Detection covers balances, limits, high limits, past due, opened/reported dates, statuses, payment status, fragments, ownership, duplicates, omissions, inquiries, and personal information. Missing data is not called an error.

Synthetic proof detects a $740 balance difference plus limit, opened/reported date, status, payment-status, duplicate, omission, and inquiry cases.
