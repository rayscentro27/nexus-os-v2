# Dispute Strategy Research Plan

## What Nexus Should Track

- Item type
- Client-selected reason
- Letter option used
- Bureau or furnisher
- Evidence uploaded
- Round number
- Response time
- Response result
- Next recommended action

## Outcome Measurement

- Deleted
- Corrected
- Verified
- Updated
- No response
- Needs escalation
- Client evidence needed
- Not sent

## Learning Method

Nexus should compare outcome categories by item type, reason, option type, bureau/furnisher, and evidence availability. Until enough real outcomes exist, recommendations remain deterministic and specialist-reviewed.

## Later External Review

- FCRA / CFPB consumer dispute guidance
- Bureau dispute process documentation
- Furnisher direct dispute guidance
- Certified mailing / DocuPost operational requirements
- GoClear internal outcome history

## Guardrails

- No promised removals or score outcomes.
- No auto-send.
- Specialist review and client approval remain required.
- No SSN, full DOB, full EIN, full account numbers, or bureau credentials collected in the portal.
