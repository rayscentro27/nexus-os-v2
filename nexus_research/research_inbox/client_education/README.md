# Client Education Research Inbox

**Owner**: Nexus Credit & Funding Research Engine  
**Purpose**: Collect and store client-safe educational content

## Rules
- No secrets, no raw client data, no production credentials
- No fake funding claims, no fake approval claims, no legal guarantees
- Research is evidence, not automatic advice
- All content requires Ray Review before client-facing
- Content must be educational, not advisory
- Must include appropriate disclaimers
- No specific credit score promises

## Categories
- Credit report education (how to read, what factors matter)
- Credit utilization education
- Business credit vs personal credit education
- Fundability education
- Business setup education
- Grant education
- Lending process education
- Credit monitoring education
- Dispute process education (general, not specific advice)
- Financial literacy education
