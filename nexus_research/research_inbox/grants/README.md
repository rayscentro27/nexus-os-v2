# Grants Research Inbox

**Owner**: Nexus Credit & Funding Research Engine  
**Purpose**: Collect and store grant opportunity research

## Rules
- No secrets, no raw client data, no production credentials
- No fake funding claims, no fake approval claims, no legal guarantees
- Research is evidence, not automatic advice
- Outputs are draft-only until approved by Ray Review
- Client-facing content requires compliance review
- No grant application guarantees
- Must verify grant legitimacy before recommending

## Categories
- Federal grant databases (grants.gov)
- State/local grant programs
- Minority business grants
- Women business grants
- Veteran business grants
- Small business grants
- Startup grants
- Industry-specific grants
- Community development grants
- Nonprofit grants
