Summary:
This research note is for Nexus/GoClear internal use to support credit readiness reviews. The goal is to help identify practical, client-safe recommendations for improving credit utilization without making score-increase guarantees or promising funding approvals.

Key points:
- Credit utilization should be reviewed as part of the overall credit readiness profile.
- High revolving utilization can reduce readiness for funding and credit applications.
- Utilization recommendations should focus on education, planning, and safer debt-management steps.
- Nexus should avoid guaranteeing score increases, deletions, or approvals.
- Recommendations should be reviewed by Ray before becoming client-facing.
- Client-facing language should explain general readiness concepts, not promise outcomes.

Compliance cautions:
- Do not guarantee credit score increases.
- Do not guarantee funding approval.
- Do not claim debts or negative items will be removed.
- Do not provide legal advice.
- Do not recommend misleading or fraudulent credit behavior.
- Do not send automated client instructions without approval.

Intended Nexus use:
Use this as a first approved artifact to test the Nexus Credit & Funding Research Adapter v1. It should route to the credit utilization / scorecard recommendation workflow and generate admin-only draft notes plus a Ray Review draft recommendation.
