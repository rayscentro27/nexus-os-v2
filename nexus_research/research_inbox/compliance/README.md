# Compliance Research Inbox

**Owner**: Nexus Credit & Funding Research Engine  
**Purpose**: Collect and store compliance notes for credit/funding operations

## Rules
- No secrets, no raw client data, no production credentials
- No fake funding claims, no fake approval claims, no legal guarantees
- Research is evidence, not automatic advice
- Outputs are admin-only until approved by Ray Review
- Compliance notes require legal review before client-facing use
- No legal advice without proper licensing

## Categories
- FCRA compliance notes
- FDCPA compliance notes
- Credit repair disclosure requirements
- Affiliate disclosure requirements (FTC)
- State credit repair licensing requirements
- Data privacy requirements
- Client consent requirements
- Record retention requirements
- Advertising compliance
- Email marketing compliance (CAN-SPAM)
