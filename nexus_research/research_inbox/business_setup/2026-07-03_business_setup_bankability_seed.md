Summary:
Internal note covering business entity setup fundamentals that support bankability and fundability readiness. This is educational-only and does not provide legal or tax advice.

Key points:
- LLC or corporation formation is a foundational step for business credibility.
- EIN (Employer Identification Number) registration is required for most business banking and credit.
- Business address should be a dedicated business location, not a residential address when possible.
- Business phone line establishes separate business identity.
- Website, domain, and professional email support legitimacy.
- NAICS code selection should reflect actual business activity — incorrect codes can cause issues.
- Business bank account should be opened after entity formation and EIN registration.
- Time in business matters — newer entities have fewer funding options.
- No claims that setup alone guarantees funding approval.
