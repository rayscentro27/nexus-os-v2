# Nexus Content & Creative Automation Policy

See [NEXUS_AUTOMATION_LEVELS.md](NEXUS_AUTOMATION_LEVELS.md). Covers Content Opportunity Lab,
Creative Studio, Design Library, and Email / SMS / DM / Social.

- **Level 1 (autonomous):** content opportunity scoring, experiment cards, draft creative
  generation, internal review cards, design library organization, draft messages, internal reports.
- **Level 2 (approval-gated):** publishing content/creative, sending content to clients, sending
  email/SMS/DM, social posting, campaign publishing, ad creative activation, scheduler activation.
- **Level 3 (blocked):** bulk send, spam automation, external AI on sensitive/customer data, spend
  on paid creative tools.

Drafting, scoring, and organizing are internal and autonomous. Every publish/send/social action is
gated behind Ray approval. Bulk send and spam automation are hard-blocked.
