# Nexus Affiliate Opportunity Tracker

`scripts/research/affiliate_opportunity_tracker.py` tracks affiliate and partner opportunities from manual CSV/sample inputs.

Command:

```bash
python3 scripts/research/affiliate_opportunity_tracker.py --dry-run --limit 5 --json
```

It does not connect live dashboards or claim terms are current unless manually proofed.

Categories include business credit, funding tools, business formation, website builders, email/newsletter, AI tools, SEO tools, bookkeeping/accounting, credit monitoring, and allowed trading education/tools.
