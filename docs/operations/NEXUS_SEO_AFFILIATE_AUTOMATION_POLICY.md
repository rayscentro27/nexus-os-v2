# Nexus SEO & Affiliate Automation Policy

See [NEXUS_AUTOMATION_LEVELS.md](NEXUS_AUTOMATION_LEVELS.md). Covers SEO / Marketing and Affiliate
Marketing.

- **Level 1 (autonomous):** SEO keyword scoring, affiliate opportunity scoring, partner research,
  internal cards/reports.
- **Level 2 (approval-gated):** publishing to site, site/production change, publishing affiliate
  content, outbound partner contact, scheduler activation.
- **Level 3 (blocked):** production deploy of site changes, broad scraping of partner/competitor
  sites, spend/commitment actions, external AI on sensitive data.

Scoring and opportunity research run autonomously and feed internal cards. Anything that publishes,
contacts a partner, or changes the live site is gated behind Ray approval. Broad scraping is
blocked — only local samples and metadata placeholders are used.
