# Nexus Content Opportunity Lab

`scripts/research/content_opportunity_lab.py` creates content opportunity cards from safe keyword fixtures/manual imports.

Command:

```bash
python3 scripts/research/content_opportunity_lab.py --dry-run --limit 5 --no-external-ai --json
```

Content types include blog posts, SEO landing pages, YouTube scripts, short-form videos, social carousels, lead magnets, email sequences, workshop outlines, and affiliate review articles.

No publishing occurs.
