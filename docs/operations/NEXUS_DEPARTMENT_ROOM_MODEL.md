# Nexus Department Room Model

Nexus OS v2 is organized as an automated company with department rooms. Command Center is Ray's Executive Office; every other tab is a room that shows live work items, recommendations, outputs, and next actions.

## Department Map

| Department | Purpose | Work item type | Data sources | Background feeds | What Ray sees | Hermes advises on | Safe actions | Approval-required actions | Next automation source |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Command Center / Executive Office | Top-floor overview of all departments. | Department summaries and decisions. | `research_sources`, `task_requests`, `approvals`, `agent_jobs`, `nexus_events`, department tables. | Manual watch report, process health refresh, department project mappers. | Department cards, active/review/blocked/scheduled counts, last update, top recommendation. | What needs review, what is blocked, what is scheduled next, what Ray should approve/change/park today. | Open department, read proof, ask Hermes. | Any command execution, scheduler activation, publish/send/trade/deploy. | Hermes memory consolidation and process registry refresh. |
| Source Intake / Research Intake Department | Instant intake for public links, YouTube URLs, pasted text, and research notes. | Research source/project. | `research_sources`, `intake_events`, `transcript_reviews`, `task_requests`, `nexus_events`. | Capture worker, YouTube monitor, transcript review, NotebookLM enrichment if connected. | Saved source cards, status, score, category, destination, pending enrichment. | Metadata review, usefulness, destination, risks, next safe research step. | Save source, analyze, create report, route to Opportunity/Creative, request more research, park. | Sensitive data, broad scrape, external AI on sensitive text, risky destination. | NotebookLM source enrichment connector. |
| Opportunity Lab / Revenue Department | Turn research into money tests, offers, affiliate paths, lead magnets, and services. | Monetization opportunity. | `monetization_opportunities`, selected research sources. | Service opportunity extractor, monetization scanner. | Opportunity cards, score, money angle, smallest test. | Pros/cons, speed to cash, compliance risk, smallest low-cost test. | Analyze, create report, create task, route to creative, park/reject. | Client-facing promotion, publish/send, paid offers that require review. | Opportunity Lab monetization scanner. |
| Design Library / Design and Asset Department | Store design references, patterns, UI quality reviews, and design packets. | Design project/asset/reference. | `design_inspiration_sources`, `design_pattern_registry`, `feature_design_packets`, `ui_quality_reviews`. | Asset organizer, UI quality reviewer. | Design reference and review cards, previews when linked. | Reference use, risk of copying, suggested changes, campaign fit. | Send to Creative, create campaign task, request changes, park. | Approve asset for public/client-facing use. | Design Library asset organizer. |
| Creative Studio / Campaign Department | Produce and review campaigns, briefs, drafts, creative assets, social drafts, and readiness packages. | Creative project/draft/asset. | `creative_assets`, `creative_campaigns`, `creative_briefs`, `creative_scores`, publish package tables. | Creative idea generator, design variant scripts, scoring scripts. | Draft assets, scores, hooks, readiness links. | Creative quality, compliance, audience fit, CTA, revision plan. | Generate draft, revise, generate social draft, send to design, park. | Publish/send/client-facing approval. | Creative idea/project generator. |
| SEO / Marketing / Growth Department | Manage content and growth opportunities. | SEO/content project. | `seo_sites`, `seo_opportunities`, research sources. | SEO checks, keyword/page scanners. | Growth cards, keywords, pages, scores, next content action. | Keyword/page priority, article/report plan, risks. | Create article draft, create report, request more research, route to creative, park. | Scheduling, publishing, client-facing claims. | SEO/marketing opportunity scanner. |
| Ops & Improvements / System Improvement Department | Improve Nexus itself and surface operational issues. | Improvement candidate/process item. | `improvement_candidates`, `ops_incidents`, `nexus_events`, system health. | Watch loop, self-audit, process health scans. | Improvement cards, incidents, pros/cons, recommendation. | Risk, implementation value, smallest safe task, schedule proposal. | Create implementation task, dry-run plan, request more research, park. | Deploy, local command, scheduler activation, raw v1 execution. | Ops self-audit and process registry refresh. |
| Agent Jobs / Automation Workforce Department | Show automation work and worker status. | Job/process/task request. | `agent_jobs`, `agent_registry`, `task_requests`, `nexus_events`. | Nexus runner, process registry refresh. | Jobs, statuses, outputs, proof links. | Failure reason, dry-run next step, whether to create task or approval. | Rerun dry-run request, create task, view proof, park. | Scheduler activation, raw local/v1 command, real publish/send/trade/deploy. | Process Registry refresh. |
| Approvals / Decision Desk | Human decision gate. | Approval item. | `approvals`, linked `task_requests`, linked preview tables. | Any risky action request. | Pending approvals, preview, risk, approve/reject/request changes. | Read-only risk explanation and recommendation. | Read, request changes, reject, ledger-only approval. | This tab records approval; downstream action gates still apply. | Universal action policy. |
| Events Feed / Proof and History Ledger | Proof/history ledger. | Proof event. | `nexus_events`. | All meaningful UI/workflow events. | History of actions and outputs. | Cite proof and explain history. | Read proof. | None directly; risky replay requires approval. | Watch report and department actions. |
| Integrations / Connections Department | Show connection names and status without exposing secrets. | Integration status item. | `integration_registry`, `model_providers`, health rows. | Status refresh only. | Registered/connected/not connected status. | What is available and what is missing. | Read status. | Enabling OAuth/API keys/schedulers. | Integration status refresh. |
| Trading Lab / Demo Research Department | Strategy research only. Live trading is blocked. | Demo strategy/research candidate. | `trading_strategy_candidates`, `trading_risk_rules`. | Future demo scanner only. | Strategy and risk cards, read-only. | Research quality and risk. | Read, park, create research task. | Trade, live execution, funded trading, performance claims. | Demo-only research collector. |

The visible UI must show department work, recommendations, summaries, outputs, and next actions. Queues, workers, and schedulers remain implementation details except where a technical status panel is intentionally collapsed.
