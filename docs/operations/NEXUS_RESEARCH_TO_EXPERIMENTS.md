# Nexus Research To Experiments

`scripts/research/research_to_experiment.py` converts strong research ideas into experiment candidates.

Command:

```bash
python3 scripts/research/research_to_experiment.py --dry-run --limit 5 --no-external-ai --json
```

Experiment fields: hypothesis, audience, offer/content angle, traffic source, expected result, test cost, test duration, success metric, next action, risk level, department owner, source research id, and priority score.

No experiment is executed by this script.
