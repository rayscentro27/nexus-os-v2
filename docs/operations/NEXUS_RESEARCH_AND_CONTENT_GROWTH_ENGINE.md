# Nexus Research And Content Growth Engine

The Research + Content Growth Engine turns watched resources, YouTube transcripts, affiliate programs, SEO keywords, research findings, experiments, content opportunities, and content tests into Nexus project cards.

## Topics

- business credit
- business funding
- grants
- online business opportunities
- AI tools
- AI automation

- affiliate marketing
- trading strategies, paper-only
- content monetization

## Safety

All scripts are dry-run by default. They do not activate schedulers, scrape broadly, capture media, run yt-dlp, call external AI, publish, send, trade, deploy, connect brokers, or touch v1 workers.

## Departments

Outputs route to Source Intake, Opportunity Lab, SEO / Marketing, Creative Studio, GoClear / Apex, Ops & Improvements, Trading Lab paper-only, and Command Center visibility.

## Schedule State

The watched resource watch path is schedule-ready but disabled. Scheduler activation requires Ray approval and a separate pass.
