# Nexus Hermes YouTube Prep Brief

`scripts/research/generate_hermes_youtube_prep_brief.py` prepares deterministic context for Hermes.

It helps Hermes answer:

- top YouTube videos/items this week
- strongest channel
- recommended direction
- GoClear SEO content candidates
- affiliate opportunities
- AI/automation ideas
- paper-only trading research
- patterns to replicate
- what Ray can ignore

No external AI is called in this pass.
