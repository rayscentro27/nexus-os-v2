# Outcome observations, not causation

Nexus stores structured report-to-report observations and client workflow milestones. A later report can show an account not found, a changed balance, status, ownership, or no measurable change. None of these records establishes that a strategy caused a reporting, score, or funding outcome. Low-confidence alignment is recorded as an uncertain comparison, never as a deletion.

Only server-side workers may create comparison records. Clients can read their own client-safe history through RLS; they cannot alter canonical accounts, discrepancies, strategy approval, or comparison facts. Mail and DocuPost are outside this workflow.
